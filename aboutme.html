About Me

Hello everyone! Welcome to my website! I am Maria Arroyo and I am from Cordoba (Spain). I moved on my own to New York City seven years ago and I am really glad I did because it has changed my life completely. I am a senior here at Baruch College, and I will be graduating this semester with a degree in Computer Information Systems and a minor in Communication Studies. I am constantly amazed about the power of technology and its ability to transform and improve our lives, and the critical part design plays in doing so.

I am currently working part time as a Junior QA Analyst for a healthcare startup called Blink Health. I really enjoy working for this company, they are always involved in new projects and I am constantly learning new things. For this reason, I would love to keep gaining experience at Blink Health after my graduation in May. In addition, I find my job very rewarding because I am part of an excellent team that is contributing to make healthcare affordable for everyone, we are helping people every day.

Some of my hobbies are attending to concerts, visiting art exhibitions, trying new restaurants, and meeting new people. I also enjoy going to the gym, swimming, dancing, and horse riding. Last, I have to say that now that my graduation day is approaching, I feel very accomplished. It was a long ride and not an easy one, I gave up a lot of things to make it here. I am very excited about graduation and very proud of myself. I stay positive because my family and friends have always encouraged me to follow my dreams and I live in a city full of opportunities that definitely have made me a stronger, ambitious, and independent woman today.
